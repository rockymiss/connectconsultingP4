WHO are we?

Connect Consulting was created and founded by Hugh Murray, one of the cofounders and directors of Classic Drinks. It stemmed from a huge desire to help businesses to grow and reach their full potential. It was going to solely focused on “Art of creative service” After working with a number of clients, it was quickly seen that businesses were looking for more help in lines of Strategic Planning & Creative Thinking, Sales and Marketing. At Connect Consulting we help our clients identify their issues, develop solutions and take remedial action. Its about thinking strategically and acting decisively. We help our clients achieve better business results through innovative thinking and problem solving. We create a meaningful impact in the lives of our clients. What sets us apart is a real genuine passion for helping others. People are thoroughly the link to a successful business. It’s about the real understanding that Customer Service starts with your staff then suppliers and then the customers. With Connect Consulting with really do connect the dots of business through people.
